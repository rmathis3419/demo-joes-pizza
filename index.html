About Mama Rosa's Kitchen

Mama Rosa's Kitchen has been serving authentic Italian cuisine to the Springfield community for nearly four decades. What started as a small family restaurant has grown into a beloved local institution.

Every dish is made with love using recipes passed down through generations, fresh ingredients, and the same passion that Mama Rosa brought to her own kitchen.
